                            SCOPE OF SUPPLY
“Eicher Engines” Powered PINNACLE Silent Diesel Generating Set shall comprise of the following:

DIESEL ENGINE:
‘Eicher Engines’ branded (manufactured by TAFE Motors & Tractors Limited) engine model 198ES,1cylinder, 4 stroke cycle, Air cooled, developing 11.7 BHP at 1500 RPM under NTP conditions of BS: 5514. The engine shall be provided with electrical starting arrangement & shall give the electrical output of 7.5 KVA at 0.8 power factor. The engine is complete with:

  • Starter motor.
  • Battery charging alternator.
  • Dry type air cleaner.
  •  MICO fuel pump.
  •  A1 class governing.
  •  Flywheel with flywheel housing.
  •  Residential silencer.
  •  First fill of lube oil.
  •  Engine protections: Low lube oil pressure(LLOP) & High Cylinder Head temperature(HCT).

ALTERNATOR:
Suitable make (Crompton Greaves), I phase, 50 cycles, self-regulated, self-excited, screen protected, 230 Volts, 2 Wire, alternator of rated capacity at 0.8 power factor. The alternator will conform to relevant BS/IS standard. The choice of alternator is to us unless specifically agreed upon.


CONTROL PANEL:
Standard/customized, louvered, steel construction Control panel, dust & vermin proof comprising of MCB/MCCB, CTs for metering, Voltmeter, Ammeter, Frequency meter, Indications, Fuses etc.


ESSENTIAL ACCESSORIES:

Fuel tank with drain valve, breather, inlet and outlet connections.
Battery with leads.
Anti vibration mountings(AVMs).
Operator manuals & test certificates.
